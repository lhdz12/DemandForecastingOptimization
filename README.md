This project works with the NYC Government database from November 2025, that contains a vast variety of yellow taxi trips carried out during this time period. The objective is to analyze current revenue and demand for these taxis during this month and identify trends. Based on that information, revenue will be optiimized and a forecast for the demand of these urban mobility services will be done.  

## 1. Business objective

The ultimate objectives are to identify trends, analyze demand and optimize revenue for yellow taxis in New York City. Based on this, the following business questions will be resolved through data analysis and engineering: 

- Which pick up zones generate highest revenue? 
- What hours produce maximum profit? 
- What is the average revenue per mile? 
- How many hours do all taxis work every day? 
- What is demand volatility per zone?
